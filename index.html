Welcome to Learn The Lingo!

The Learn The Lingo Game is a fun and interative game for young children to take their first steps into learning Spanish. The game is recommended for children between the ages of 3 and 8. Learning a second language at an early age can boost problem solving and listening skills as well as allowing children to connect with other cultures.
Learn The Lingo Game will give your child the opportunity to start to learn basic Spanish words that will give them the base to learn further as they get older.

Instructions on how to play

The Learning the Lingo game is simple to play.

Firstly select the Play Game button.

In the next page you see a question and 3 possible answers. Select the answer you think is correct and a message will appear letting you know if you are correct or not.

Once you complete the 10 questions, select the Check Your Score button and your score will be revealed to you.

Enjoy playing our little language learning game. Play again by selecting the home button in the top right hand corner and then in the home page select the Play Game button. Another set of question will be available for you to answer and test your Spanish skills!
